🎭 Tips for accurate mood tracking:

Quick Start Guide

  1. Choose a theme that resonates with your style (Classic Emoji, Meme, Cartoon, etc.)
  2. Set a daily reminder for mood check-ins
  3. Select the face that best matches your current emotional state
  4. Add optional notes to provide context for your mood
  5. Review your mood patterns in the weekly summary

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start creating my theme?

Browse existing themes for inspiration, prepare your mood faces, and use our submission form. We recommend starting with a 6×6 grid for your first theme.

What types of faces can I include?

We welcome all types - emojis, memes, cartoons, original artwork, and more! Just ensure they're appropriate and express clear emotions.

How long does approval take?

Our team reviews submissions within 24-48 hours. We'll notify you via email once your theme is approved and live!
